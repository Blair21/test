Gal Gadot

Israeli actress and model

At age 18, she was crowned Miss Israel 2004.

Career

  • Gadot's first international film role came as Gisele Yashar in Fast & Furious (2009)
  • She went on to achieve global stardom for portraying Wonder Woman in the DC Extended Universe, including in Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (2016) and Wonder Woman (2017).
  • starred in the Netflix action comedy film Red Notice (2021) and the mystery film Death on the Nile (2022).
